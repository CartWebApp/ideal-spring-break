Chris Andrade's Ideal Spring Break

  My ideal spring break would include mostly video games and sleep (basically a week-long version of what I already do on weekends). I've been meaning to get back into Destiny, so ideally, I would play enough Destiny to get my light level up to 650. I would also try to get around to some of the stuff that I've been neglecting around the house, like organizing the cables behind my TV or completely rewiring my computer.

There are also about a dozen books I've been meaning to get to that a week-long break would give me the opportunity to read. They're mostly lore for the World of Warcraft video game series, which I find incredibly interesting. Books set in a video game universe are nearly the only genre I'm interested in reading, so I burn through them in a matter of 2-3 days, then have to find another. One author in particular, Christie Golden, writes amazing books for the Warcraft series, such as Arthas, Tides of War, and Before the Storm.
